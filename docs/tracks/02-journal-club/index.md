# Journal Club

This track prepares you to participate in and lead a journal club.
You will learn how to present a paper clearly, structure a discussion, and give and receive constructive feedback.

!!! note "Under construction"
    Lesson content is being developed. The outline below shows what each lesson will cover.

## Lessons

- **Lesson 01 — How to Present a Paper**: Slide structure, choosing what to include, and managing time.
- **Lesson 02 — Leading a Discussion**: Prompts, pacing, and handling questions you cannot answer.
- **Lesson 03 — Giving Feedback**: How to critique constructively as an audience member.
