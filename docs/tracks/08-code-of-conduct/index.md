# Code of Conduct

A code of conduct is the written agreement that defines how a community treats its members.
It sets expectations for behaviour, explains what is not acceptable, and describes what happens
when someone is harmed.

This track explains why codes of conduct exist, what the Nestling community expects of its members,
and how to act, or get help, when something goes wrong.

A healthy research environment does not happen by accident.
It is built by people who take shared standards seriously and act on them when they need to.
