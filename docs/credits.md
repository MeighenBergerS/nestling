# Credits

Nestling builds on materials and ideas from the following projects and people.
We are grateful to everyone who made their work openly available.

---

## Plotting

The Nestling matplotlib style and the plotting track draw heavily from:

**HowToMakeAPlot** by Ciaran O'Hare
: A guide to making publication-quality scientific figures with matplotlib.
  Repository: <https://github.com/cajohare/HowToMakeAPlot>

---

## ArXiv & Journal Club

The ArXiv track and journal club materials are adapted from:

**jc-ppi** by Stephan Meighen-Berger
: A repository for journal club paper presentations and ArXiv paper tooling.
  Repository: <https://github.com/MeighenBergerS/jc-ppi>

---

## Writing

The writing track style conventions are based on:

**SMB Style Guidelines** by Stephan Meighen-Berger
: A detailed writing style guide derived from published physics papers.

---

## Coding Practices & Documentation Style

The coding track structure, docstring conventions, and MkDocs configuration are adapted from:

**Prometheus** by Stephan Meighen-Berger and contributors
: A Python-based neutrino detector simulation package with extensive documentation.
  Repository: <https://github.com/Harvard-Neutrino/prometheus>
